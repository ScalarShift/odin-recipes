Description

Lasagna, one of humanity's greatest creations. Layers of firm but soft noodles, a garlicy tomato sauce, and texture-rich ricotta cheese, all topped with a blanket of stringy mozzarella make this dish second to none.

Ingredients

  • 2 heaps of lasagna noodles
  • 6 sizeable slooges of tomato sauce
  • 1/8 of a shit-ton (metric) of ricotta cheese
  • 1 mid-sized mountain of mozzarella cheese

Steps

  • 1.Bring to a boil enough water to cover the noodles with an inch spare
  • 2.Add the noodles and boil about 10 minutes or until al dente
  • 3.In a baking dish of desired size, layer the noodles, sauce, and ricotta until the dish is full
  • 4.Top with mozzarella, preheat the oven to hot and bake for a long ass time until the top is golden brown
  • 5.Enjoy both the food and the coma it brings on
