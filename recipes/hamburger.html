Description

Another of humanity's most delicious creations, the humble hamburger is an icon of American cuisine. With its classic combination of juicy ground-beef patty, crispy onions, and melty cheese among other fabulous toppings, this dish will leave you craving more.

Ingredients

  • An amount of crumpled cow
  • Enough slices of fermented milk curds (your choice)
  • A small salad bar of salad-bar-related toppings
  • 2 buns, a top and a bottom
  • One galore of condiments

Steps

  • 1.Press the crumpled cow into patties of desired size
  • 2.Put the patties on something hot enough to really burn you and cook until cooked
  • 3.Transfer the cow patties onto the bottom bun (or the top for an Australia burger)
  • 4.Top with cheese, salad, condiments, and finally the other bun
  • 5.Make sure to fire a gun into the air if it's July 4th and dig in!
